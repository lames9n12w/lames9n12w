Attack on Titan – The main Characters

Eren Yeager:

Role: Initially, Eren is the primary protagonist, driven by a burning desire for freedom and revenge against the Titans after his mother's death. Over time, he transforms into the Attack Titan, and his motivations become more complex, leading him to initiate the Rumbling, a catastrophic event aimed at freeing Eldians but with a high human cost.


Mikasa Ackerman:

Role: Eren's adoptive sister, Mikasa is one of the central characters, known for her exceptional combat skills. She is fiercely protective of Eren, often acting as his guardian. Her role evolves from a close ally to someone who must confront Eren's actions in the final chapters, ultimately making the decision to end his life to stop the Rumbling.


Armin Arlert:

Role: Eren's childhood friend and Mikasa's close companion, Armin is initially less physically capable but compensates with his intelligence and strategic thinking. He becomes a key strategist for the Survey Corps and eventually inherits the power of the Colossal Titan. In the end, he leads the resistance against Eren and plays a significant role in negotiating peace post-war.


Connie Springer:

Role: A member of the 104th Training Corps, Connie is known for his humor and loyalty to his friends. He plays a supporting role in the series, often providing comic relief and emotional support to his comrades. His role becomes more significant in the later seasons as he grapples with the consequences of the Rumbling.


Sasha Blouse:

Role: Another member of the 104th Training Corps, Sasha is known for her love of food and sharpshooting skills. She provides a light-hearted presence in the series, often diffusing tense situations with her humor. Her role takes a tragic turn when she dies in a battle against the Marleyan forces.


Jean Kirstein:

Role: Initially a rival to Eren, Jean matures into a key member of the Survey Corps, known for his leadership and tactical skills. He plays a crucial role in the series, often providing a voice of reason and challenging Eren's decisions. His role becomes more prominent in the later seasons as he grapples with the complexities of war and morality.


Levi Ackerman:

Role: Known as "Humanity's Strongest Soldier," Levi is a member of the Survey Corps and later its captain. Throughout the series, he's pivotal in numerous Titan battles, acts as a mentor to many, and has a significant personal vendetta against Zeke Yeager. His role includes critical confrontations and leadership, especially after the death of Erwin Smith.


Erwin Smith:

Role: The 13th commander of the Survey Corps, Erwin is a strategic mastermind whose decisions drive the plot forward. His leadership and sacrifices are central to humanity's fight against the Titans. He meets his end in a crucial battle, leaving a legacy that shapes the series' direction.


Hange Zoë:

Role: Succeeds Erwin as the commander of the Survey Corps. Hange is passionate about Titan research and a key figure in understanding Titan mechanics. Their role involves leading the corps, making tough decisions, and ultimately sacrificing themselves during the final battle against Eren.


Floch Forster:

Role: Initially a minor character, Floch becomes a significant figure in the series, representing the radical faction of the Survey Corps. His loyalty to Eren and his extremist views on Eldian supremacy lead to violent confrontations and a pivotal role in the Rumbling. His character exemplifies the moral ambiguity and complexity of the series.


Reiner Braun:

Role: Introduced as a friend to Eren and others, Reiner is later revealed as the Armored Titan, creating a moral and emotional conflict. His role shifts from antagonist to a more nuanced character caught between his duties to Marley and his connections to Paradis.


Annie Leonhart:

Role: Known as the Female Titan, Annie starts as a seemingly cold but skilled soldier in the 104th Training Corps. Her true allegiance to Marley and her capture by the Survey Corps are key plot points. She reemerges later with a more complex role in the narrative.


Zeke Yeager:

Role: Eren's half-brother and the Beast Titan, Zeke has a significant impact on the story, manipulating events from behind the scenes. His philosophies and plans regarding Eldian freedom or destruction are central to the conflict in later seasons.


Historia Reiss:

Role: Initially known as Krista Lenz, Historia is revealed to be the true heir to the throne of the Reiss family. Her role evolves from a passive character to the queen of Paradis, influencing political decisions and humanizing the narrative with her compassion.

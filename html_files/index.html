Attack on Titan – A Dark Tale of Survival

Welcome to the Attack on Titan fan page! This movie, based on the famous anime and manga, takes us into a world where humanity fights for survival against massive humanoid creatures known as Titans. With intense action, deep storytelling, and shocking twists, Attack on Titan remains one of the most thrilling dystopian tales ever created.


"Attack on Titan" is a gripping dystopian anime film based on the acclaimed manga and TV series. Set in a world where the remnants of humanity live within cities surrounded by enormous walls to protect them from giant humanoid creatures called Titans, the story follows Eren Yeager, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armin Arlert. Their lives are upended when a colossal Titan breaches their wall, leading to a desperate battle for survival. The film delves into themes of freedom, vengeance, and the harsh realities of war, as the trio and their comrades fight not just for survival but to unravel the mysteries of the Titans' origin and purpose.
